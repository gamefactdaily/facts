Hey there, I'm Dario.

I am a web developer and designer from Texas. I am passionately obsessed with technology, infinitely curious and insatiably thirsty for knowledge. I am prominently known for my site iEmulators, my help on beta testing GBA4iOS and other gaming-related projects and designs.


I am known as mithical nearly everywhere online.

Origins

I was born in Monterrey, Mexico and lived there until my family moved to Texas. My heritage is of Hispanic descent and I'm fluent in both English and Spanish. I went online for the first time in 2005 and haven't logged off since. I frequented several online communities where I slowly developed a love for coding and photoshop and all things Apple.

Education

I graduated as an IT computer and internet specialist but I was always drawn to web development, design and coding. One day I decided to purchase some online guides and tutorials and teach myself HTML and CSS. To this day I continue learning new computer languages that can help me achieve my goals.

Hobbies

Some of my hobbies include DSLR and analog photography, reading many kinds of books, watching several shows and movies on Netflix, gaming on multiple consoles, traveling the country, collecting records, buying antiques, Pokémon shiny hunting and VGC tournaments, going to conventions and concerts with friends and learning new ways to better myself everyday.
