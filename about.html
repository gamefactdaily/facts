Hey there, I'm Dario.

I am a web developer and designer from Texas. I am passionately obsessed with technology, social media and the internet. I am prominently known for my site iEmulators, my help on beta testing GBA4iOS and other gaming-related projects and designs you may know around the web.

I am known as mithical nearly everywhere online.

Origins

I was born in Monterrey, Mexico but grew up in Texas. I am of Hispanic descent and also fluent in English and Spanish. I went online for the first time in 2005 and haven't logged off since. Back in those days I frequented several online communities where I slowly developed a love for social media, coding, photoshop and all things Apple.

Education

I graduated as an IT computer and internet specialist but I was always drawn to web development, design and coding on the side. One day I decided to purchase some online guides and tutorials and teach myself how to code. To this day I continue learning new computer languages that can help me achieve my goals and create new projects.

Hobbies

Some of my hobbies include DSLR and analog photography, reading all kinds of books, watching shows and movies on Netflix, gaming on multiple consoles, traveling the world, collecting records, buying antiques, Pokémon shiny hunting and VGC tournaments, going to conventions and concerts with friends and learning new ways to better myself everyday.
