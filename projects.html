iEmulators

I designed, coded and launched iEmulators with the help of a some friends as a service that allows you to easily download emulators and other apps not usually found within the App Store to your phone or tablet for free. Today it is still one of the most popular download hubs for iOS emulators online.

The GBA4iOS Beta Blog was one of my most popular projects. GBA4iOSkins

Before iEmulators was even born, GBA4iOSkins was one of the first big projects I started with my team. The idea was to have a site that easily let people download controller skins for GBA4iOS and it proved to be successful for a while. This project was archived shortly after GBA4iOS was revoked and it wasn't viable to keep the site open at the time. It may still come back one day if there's demand.
