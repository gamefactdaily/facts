Shiny Pokémon

In Pokémon games there is a low chance to find alternate colored or "shiny" Pokémon while playing. This has led to the rise of Shiny Hunters, people that attempt to find and capture these elusive and rare Pokémon. Shiny hunting has always been one of my favorite aspects of the franchise and in this page I keep track of my captures.

Nintendo Switch

Pokémon has finally arrived on console after many years in the form of two non-traditional games where shiny hunting has again been included.
